HISTORY of ENHYPEN

It all started in South Korea where Belift Lab (BE:LIFT) ( which is a joint venture between the entertainment companies CJ E&M and Big Hit Entertainment.) organised the male group survival reality show called I-LAND.
The show takes place in the I-LAND, a state-of-the-art construction with 3 years in the making. Here the participants will live, train, and perform to become the perfect idol group. Those that do not perform up to standards will be sent to the GROUND, where they must rise to the challenge to survive. The show is presented by Namkoong Min. The producers of the show are Bang Si-Hyuk, global artist Rain, and genius producer Zico.

I-LAND was first released on June 26, 2020 by the South Korean channel Mnet. I-LAND auditions were held in 2019 in different cities and countries including: United States, Japan, and Korea, targeting male applicants born between 1997 and 2008. Here are a total of 23 applicants that passed the auditions: Daniel, EJ, Geonu, Hanbin, Heeseung, Jaebeom, Jaeho, Jake, Jay, Jimin, Jungwon, K, Kyungmin, Nicholas, Ni-ki, Seon, Sungchul, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Taeyong, Ta-ki, Yoonwon, and Youngbin.

However, only seven members will be able to debut in the final group. The final lineup of seven members was announced on the final episode on September 18, 2020. The winning group is called ENHYPEN.

The top six were saved after the final test of part 1 through the votes of other i-landers and producers. The bottom six were voted by global fans. While, the part 2 or the last part of the survival show the first six was decided by global fans through voting on weverse app and the last I-lander that will become a member of ENHYPEN was decided by the producers.

out of 23 I-landers Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki survived.

ENHYPEN - derives its name from the symbol Hyphen (-), that creates new meanings including "Connection, Discovery, & Growth" Similar to how a hyphen connects different words to discover new meanings, Enhypen aims "to come together to connect, discover and grow together to create a new act." The fandom name was announced on October 9,2020, Enhypen officially announced the name ENGENE which has two meanings : firstly, their fans are the "engines" that lets them grow and keep on going and secondly, the fans are the ENHYPEN's "gene" as they both share the same DNA to connect, discover, and grow together.
